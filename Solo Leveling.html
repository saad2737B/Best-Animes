Solo Leveling

About:

Solo Leveling is a globally popular Korean web novel and manhwa that follows the journey of Sung Jin-Woo, the world’s weakest hunter, as he rises to become the most powerful being alive. Set in a world where mysterious gates connect Earth to deadly dungeons, hunters are humanity’s only defense. After a near-death experience in a double dungeon, Jin-Woo gains a unique ability: the power to level up on his own — a skill no other hunter possesses. With stunning battles, emotional moments, and epic world-building, Solo Leveling has captivated fans worldwide through its thrilling storytelling, incredible artwork, and now its anime adaptation. This site is dedicated to celebrating everything Solo Leveling — from character breakdowns and episode summaries to fan theories and the latest updates. Level up your fandom. Welcome to the world of Solo Leveling.

Where To Start?

  • Season 1 (Episodes 1–12):
    • Covers Jin-Woo’s awakening in the double dungeon, the start of his leveling-up ability, and his early rise in the hunter world.

  • Season 2 (Episodes 13–24):
    • Continues right after Season 1, diving into major arcs like the Red Gate, Demon Castle, and Jin-Woo’s evolution into an elite S-rank hunter. It brings even more intensity, world-building, and unforgettable battles.

  • Manga Readers:
    • Start reading from Chapter 110. This picks up right after Season 2 ends. The manhwa continues all the way to Chapter 179, completing the full Solo Leveling storyline with epic battles, global threats, and a powerful ending.

Main Charecters:

  1. Sung Jin-Woo:
    2. The protagonist of Solo Leveling. Once known as the weakest E-rank hunter, Jin-Woo gains a mysterious system that allows him to grow stronger by leveling up — a power unique only to him. From near-death experiences to god-tier power, he becomes the Shadow Monarch.

  2. Shadow Monarch (Ashborn):
    3. The previous Shadow Monarch who chose Jin-Woo as his successor. Though he was feared as a god of death, Ashborn’s true nature and intentions reveal a much deeper connection to humanity’s survival.

  3. Yoo Jin-Ho:
    4. A kind-hearted, loyal B-rank hunter and Jin-Woo’s closest friend. Despite coming from a wealthy background, he values trust and integrity over power. He often partners with Jin-Woo and provides support during dungeon raids.

  4. Cha Hae-In:
    5. An S-rank hunter and the only one who senses something unusual about Jin-Woo. Known for her powerful swordsmanship and quiet demeanor, she becomes emotionally connected to Jin-Woo as the story unfolds.

  5. Go Gun-Hee:
    6. Chairman of the Korean Hunter Association. Wise, powerful, and respectful, he plays a key role in supporting Jin-Woo during major national-level threats and recognizes Jin-Woo’s importance early on.

  6. Thomas Andre:
    7. An American National-Level S-rank hunter and one of the world’s strongest. He initially clashes with Jin-Woo but later becomes an ally and supporter after witnessing his strength and character.

  7. Lennart Niermann:
    8. Germany’s top hunter and another National-Level hunter. Though not as prominent as Thomas, he respects Jin-Woo and plays a role during the final arcs of the series.

  8. Antares:
    9. The Monarch of Destruction and the main antagonist in the later parts of the series. His goal is to wipe out humanity, and he poses the greatest threat to Jin-Woo and the world.

  9. Sung Il-Hwan:
    10. Jin-Woo’s long-lost father, who returns with mysterious powers. His backstory and role in the greater war between Monarchs and Rulers adds emotional depth and key plot reveals.
